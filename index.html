About Me

Some of my earliest memories are of playing games on my family's NES and Sega Genesis. Once my family got our first PC when I was 5 or 6, I remember my dad showing me how to use MS Paint. My 5-year-old mind was blown.

I've been fascinated by the amazing things code can achieve ever since then and have always wanted to not only understand how but do it myself, too.

As the first step towards becoming a developer, I'm learning full-stack web development. I'm currently working on improving my html and css, and will be learning JavaScript soon.

I'm interested in a wide range of areas including web development, UI/UX design, game development, and more.
